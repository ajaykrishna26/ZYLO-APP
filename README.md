Zylo — AI-Powered Dyslexia Reading & Pronunciation Assistant

Zylo is an AI-powered mobile learning platform designed to help children, students, and individuals with dyslexia improve reading fluency, pronunciation accuracy, and language comprehension through interactive reading exercises and intelligent speech analysis.

By combining Speech Recognition, Artificial Intelligence, Text-to-Speech (TTS), and Pronunciation Assessment technologies, Zylo provides real-time feedback, personalized learning experiences, and progress tracking to support effective reading development.

🚀 Project Overview

Reading difficulties and pronunciation challenges can significantly impact learning outcomes. Zylo addresses these challenges by providing an intelligent reading assistant that listens to users as they read, evaluates pronunciation accuracy, identifies mispronounced words, and offers corrective feedback instantly.

The platform leverages advanced AI models, including OpenAI Whisper Speech Recognition, to create a highly accurate and engaging reading experience that promotes independent learning and continuous improvement.
